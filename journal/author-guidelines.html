Author Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • All submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • Articles should be submitted in HTML format (Word‑to‑HTML conversion tools are available).
  • Include a title, author name(s), abstract, keywords, main text, and references.
  • Use the IMRaD structure (Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion) for original research.

Submission Process

Submit your manuscript via email to kfcwriters@gmail.com with the subject line "Article Submission – GJMR". Attach your HTML file and a cover letter.

Review Process

All submissions undergo editorial review. Authors will receive a decision within 2–4 weeks. Accepted articles are published within one week of acceptance.
