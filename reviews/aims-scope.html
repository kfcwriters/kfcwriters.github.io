Aims & Scope

The Global Journal of Medical Research is a broad‑scope, open‑access journal dedicated to advancing knowledge across all fields of medicine. We publish original reviews, case reports, clinical studies, and research articles that contribute to the global medical community.

Areas of Interest

  • Clinical Medicine and Surgery
  • Medical Education and Writing
  • Public Health and Epidemiology
  • Pharmacology and Therapeutics
  • Medical Ethics and Professionalism
  • Healthcare Management and Policy

We welcome submissions from researchers, clinicians, and students worldwide. Our mission is to provide a platform for high‑quality, accessible medical scholarship.
